Heat Transfer Calculation in a Cylinder

Consider a hollow cylinder with inner radius r1 and outer radius r2 having thermal conduction of k w/m2-K. Obtain the conduction heat transfer rate and the temperature distribution across the cross section of the cylinder given that at the interior of the cylinder a fluid at temperature T_gas with heat transfer coefficient h_gas exists and the condition surrounding the tube is such that the temperature is T0 and heat transfer coefficient of h_ambient. The inner surface and the outer surface of the cylinder is at T_inner and T_outer respectively.
